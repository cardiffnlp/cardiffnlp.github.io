1st Cardiff NLP Summer Workshop

We are organising a free in-person workshop on Natural Language Processing.

It will take place from June 30th to July 1st 2022 in Abacws, the brand-new building for the School of Computer Scicence and the School of Mathematics at Cardiff University.

Target Audience

  • The workshop is especially intended to PhD students in UK/Europe, but everyone would be welcome!

Workshop Activities

  • Invited speakers
  • Tutorials
  • Networking

And of course you will have the chance to visit our lovely city while in Cardiff!
